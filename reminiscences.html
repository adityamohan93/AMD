Invitation for Golden Jubilee Conclave

Dear All,

The Department of Applied Mechanics, MNNIT Allahabad (Estd. in July 1964) plans to organize various programs during its Golden Jubilee year 2013-14.
As a part of it, a Golden Jubilee Conclave is planned during February 15-16, 2014 at MNNIT Allahabad.
All the present and past faculty members, staffs and students of this department are invited to attend the meet.
For this, you are requested to contact at goldenjubileeamd@gmail.com with your contact details and ideas...
You are also requested to forward this message to all known persons ever related to this department to make this Golden Jubilee Meet a memorable one!


Best Regards,
Golden Jubilee Celebration Team.
