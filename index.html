About us

This webpage is specially designed to observe the yearlong activities under the Golden Jubilee Celebration (2013-14) of the Department of Applied Mechanics at Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology Allahabad. This webpage serves as an interface among all the past and present the faculty members, staff and students of the department.



The Department of Applied Mechanics was established in July 1964. It was initially named as "Department of Applied Mechanics, Hydraulic and Hydraulic Machines", which was replaced by new name "Applied Mechanics Department" in 2003.

The disciplines

The Department offers courses at undergraduate level on Solid Mechanics, Fluid Mechanics, Hydraulic Machines, Structural Analysis, Material Science, Engineering Mechanics, Mechanics of Deformable Solids, Structures, Kinematics of Mechanics, Dynamics of Machines, Theory of plates & shells, Mechanical Vibration and Nano Technology.

The thrust area(s) of the department can be divided into two groups: Mechanics & Materials and Fluid Mechanics involving Theoretical and Experimental studies.


Composite Materials and Structures

  • Smart structures and materials
  • Nano-composites and multi-scale composites
  • Experimental and computational mechanics
  • Composite material modeling and fabrication
  • Biomaterials and biomechanics
  • Micro electro mechanical systems (MEMS)
  • Stability & Dynamics of structures
  • Multifunctional composites
  • Synthesis characterization and application of ceramic composites (ferrites, PZT etc)
  • Fabrication and testing of fiber reinforced polymer composites for specific applications
  • Acoustics

Aerodynamics

  • Experimental studies on internal and external flows
  • Computational fluid dynamics
  • Passive and active flow control
  • Turbulence modeling
  • Solid-fluid interactions
  • Applications to biological and industrial applications like human respiratory system, pump etc.
